Rugix Ctrl is designed to slot into an existing embedded stack rather than replace it. This section covers how it fits in with the rest of your toolchain.

**[Build Systems](./build-systems/)** covers getting Rugix Ctrl into your OS image, whether you use Rugix Bakery, Yocto, Buildroot, or your own pipeline.

**[Fleet Management](./fleet-management/)** covers pairing Rugix Ctrl with a fleet-management backend such as Nexigon, thin-edge.io, Memfault, or Mender.

**[Migrating](./migrating/)** covers moving to Rugix Ctrl from RAUC or Mender without reflashing devices already in the field.
